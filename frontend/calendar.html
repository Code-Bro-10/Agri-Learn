🌾 Indian Agriculture

Crop Calendar 📅

Your month-by-month guide to sowing and harvesting — tailored for Indian farmers

☀️ Kharif — Monsoon (Jun–Oct)
❄️ Rabi — Winter (Oct–Mar)
🌸 Zaid — Summer (Mar–Jun)
🗓 January
❄️ Rabi Season
🌱 Sow / Prepare
Sunflower Summer Groundnut Bitter Gourd (nursery)
🌿 Growing Strong
Wheat Barley Mustard Chickpea Peas Lentils
🌻 Harvest Now
Onion Spinach Radish Carrot
💡 Protect Rabi crops from cold waves & frost. Apply nitrogen top-dressing to wheat at tillering stage for maximum yield.
🗓 February
❄️ Rabi Season
🌱 Sow / Prepare
Cucumber (nursery) Watermelon (nursery) Bitter Gourd
🌿 Growing Strong
Wheat Mustard Chickpea Sugarcane
🌻 Harvest Now
Mustard (early) Carrot Radish Peas Onion
💡 Apply 2nd nitrogen dose to wheat. Watch for aphid attack on mustard — spray neem oil if infestation is heavy.
🗓 March
🌸 Zaid Season
🌱 Sow Now
Cucumber Watermelon Muskmelon Bitter Gourd Okra Bottle Gourd
🌻 Harvest Now
Wheat (early vars) Chickpea Lentils Peas Vegetables
💡 Temperature rises rapidly — irrigate Rabi crops well before harvest. Start summer crop nurseries & apply mulching to conserve soil moisture.
🗓 April
🌸 Zaid Season
🌱 Sow Now
Groundnut Moong (Green Gram) Cowpea Sunflower Sesame
🌻 Harvest Now
Wheat Barley Mustard Onion (main)
💡 Major Rabi harvest month! Thresh & dry crops properly before storage. Good time to plough and prepare fields for Kharif season.
🗓 May
🌸 Zaid / Pre-Kharif
🌱 Sow Now
Okra Bottle Gourd Sponge Gourd Maize (summer) Cowpea
🌻 Harvest Now
Watermelon Muskmelon Cucumber Bitter Gourd
💡 Extreme heat — provide shade nets for nurseries. Irrigate twice weekly. Begin pre-Kharif field prep: deep ploughing & manure application.
🗓 June
☀️ Kharif Season Begins
🌱 Sow Now
Rice (nursery) Maize Jowar Cotton Soybean Groundnut Sugarcane Arhar
🌻 Harvest Now
Moong (summer) Cowpea (summer)
💡 Kharif starts with monsoon onset! Prepare paddy nurseries 25–30 days before transplanting. Test soil & apply recommended fertilizers before sowing.
🗓 July
☀️ Kharif Season
🌱 Sow / Transplant
Rice (transplant) Arhar Turmeric Ginger Late Cotton Sesame
🌻 Harvest Now
Baby Corn (early) Summer Vegetables
💡 Peak transplanting month for paddy — maintain 20×15 cm spacing for better tillering. Control stem borer with pheromone traps & neem spray.
🗓 August
☀️ Kharif Season
🌱 Sow Now
Onion (Kharif) Radish Spinach
🌿 Growing Strong
Rice Maize Cotton Soybean Arhar
🌻 Harvest Now
Maize (early) Cluster Beans Cowpea
💡 Watch for leaf blast in rice — spray tricyclazole if spotted. Apply phosphorus to cotton at boll-formation stage for better yield.
🗓 September
☀️ Kharif Harvest
🌱 Sow / Prepare
Potato (early) Onion (Rabi nursery) Early Mustard (Toria) Coriander
🌻 Harvest Now
Soybean Groundnut Cotton (early) Maize
💡 Start Rabi field preparations after Kharif harvest. Apply 10–12 t/ha farmyard manure. Monsoon receding — manage drainage carefully.
🗓 October
❄️ Rabi Season Begins
🌱 Sow Now
Wheat Chickpea Mustard Onion (Rabi) Masoor (Lentil) Peas
🌻 Harvest Now
Rice Maize Soybean Groundnut
💡 Major Kharif harvest month! After harvest, begin Rabi — optimal wheat sowing window is Oct 15 to Nov 15 for highest yield.
🗓 November
❄️ Rabi Season
🌱 Sow Now
Wheat Barley Lentils Peas Late Mustard Fenugreek
🌻 Harvest Now
Cotton Late Rice Arhar Sugarcane (begins)
💡 Timely wheat sowing before Nov 15 gives 10–15% higher yield. Apply full basal dose of DAP and potash at sowing time.
🗓 December
❄️ Rabi Season
🌱 Sow Now
Cabbage Cauliflower Broccoli Coriander Spinach Garlic
🌿 Growing Strong
Wheat Mustard Chickpea Peas
🌻 Harvest Now
Carrot Radish Spinach Peas (early varieties)
💡 Cold weather — irrigate wheat at crown-root initiation stage (21 days after sowing). Use polythene covers at night to protect tender crops from frost.
