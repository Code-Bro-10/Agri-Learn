Crop Disease Library

Identify and treat common crop diseases quickly.

🔍
🦠
Late Blight
🥔 Potato & Tomato
Symptoms

Dark water-soaked spots on leaves. White fungal growth on undersides in humid conditions.

Treatment

Apply fungicides such as Mancozeb or Chlorothalonil. Avoid overhead watering.

🍂
Powdery Mildew
🥒 Cucumbers, Peas, Grapes
Symptoms

White, powdery fungal spots on leaves and stems. Leaves may turn yellow and drop off.

Treatment

Spray neem oil or sulfur-based organic fungicides. Ensure good air circulation.

🌾
Yellow Rust
🌾 Wheat
Symptoms

Yellowish-orange pustules arranged in stripes on leaves. Severely impacts yield.

Treatment

Spray Propiconazole or Tebuconazole as soon as identified. Use resistant seed varieties.

⚫
Black Spot
🌹 Roses & Fruit Trees
Symptoms

Circular black spots on leaves with fringed edges. Leaves eventually turn yellow and drop.

Treatment

Remove infected leaves. Spray copper fungicide or a baking soda solution every 7 days.

🐛
Fall Armyworm
🌽 Maize & Sorghum
Symptoms

Massive defoliation. Larvae eat through the whorl of the plant causing "window pane" damage.

Treatment

Use pheromone traps. Spray Spinetoram or Emamectin Benzoate inside the whorl.

🍌
Panama Disease
🍌 Banana
Symptoms

Yellowing of older leaves, starting at the margins. Stems split and roots rot.

Treatment

Soil-borne fungus. No chemical cure. Burn infected plants and plant resistant varieties like Grand Naine.
